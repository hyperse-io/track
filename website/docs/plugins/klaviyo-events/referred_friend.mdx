If you’d like to report on who’s referred your brand to a friend or send a thank you note to the person who referred you, you can track Referred Friend and Referred by Friend events. Similar to when someone shares content with a friend.

## Parameters

### recipients

<Property /> `string[]`

The article recipient(s), identified by their email address.

### quantity

<Property /> `number`

The quantity of articles shared.
